From Obollo-Afor to All of Nigeria

IG NICE & SONS was founded over 15 years ago at No. 4 Old Oturukpo Road, Obollo-Afor, Enugu State, with one simple goal: to give every Nigerian access to the comfort they deserve at a price they can afford.

From a single shop, we've grown into one of the most recognized Mouka Foam distributors in Enugu State — serving individual families, hotels, hospitals, schools, dormitories, and corporate institutions.

As a fully authorized Mouka distributor, every product we carry comes direct from the manufacturer with a genuine warranty. We never stock counterfeits — our reputation is built entirely on trust.
