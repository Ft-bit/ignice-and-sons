From Obollo-Afor to All of Nigeria

IG NICE & SONS was founded over 15 years ago in the heart of Obollo-Afor, Enugu State, with one simple goal: to give every Nigerian access to the comfort they deserve at a price they can afford.

Starting from a single shop on Old Oturukpo Road, we have grown into one of the most recognized Mouka Foam distributors in Enugu State — serving individual families, hotels, hospitals, schools, dormitories, and corporate institutions.

As a fully authorized Mouka distributor, every product we carry comes direct from the manufacturer with a genuine warranty. We never stock counterfeit products — our reputation is built on trust.

Over 10,000 customers have trusted us with their comfort. We deliver same-day within Obollo-Afor and ship nationally. Our team is always available to guide you to the right product for your budget and sleep style.
